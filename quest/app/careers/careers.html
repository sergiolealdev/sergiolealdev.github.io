Quest is one of the Irish companies leading the e- business revolution. We work with some of the most interesting companies in the European & North American markets and combined with our focus on the leading areas of technology, our employees are always being challenged with their knowledge and skills.

Our core values are those of Quality, Reliability, Professionalism and Participation by all and we actively encourage people to develop their business skills and to become a pro-active member of the company.

The greatest asset any company has is its people and at Quest we strive to recruit and retain the best in the industry. Project managers have on average 8-10 years industry experience whilst our team leaders and senior developers have an average of 5-6 years industry experience.

Our philosophy of continuous training and development has resulted in a broad knowledge base around our core Oracle skill-set. Project methodologies are driven by our Quality Management System, under the guidance of ISO-9001 and TickIT, to which we have been accredited for more than 10 years.

Due to continuing expansion, Quest are constantly looking for skilled business & IT people to join our team. Please refer to our Current Vacancies page for details of current job opportunities.

If you combine excellent technical skills, are customer focused and believe that you can add value to the company please send your details to careers@quest.ie
