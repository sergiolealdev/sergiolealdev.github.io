Established in 1989 – Quest has grown to become a leading Business Information System specialist, having built its reputation on designing and developing mission critical solutions for private and public sector organisations.

Specialising in Knowledge based solutions, Quest believes strongly in the power of the internet as a business enabler, and specialises in combining this technology with its expertise in Relational databases.

Knowledge Management & Collaborative Working are the cornerstones to our business approach and this is reflected in the range of Solutions, Services and Consultancy that the company offers. Quest also provides a fully hosted Managed Services offering for our clients.

Quest’s clients are drawn from a wide range of industry and market sectors across Europe and North America. These include Banking & Finance, Leisure, Local and Central Government, Charities and Foundations, Manufacturing and the Services sector.

Quest’s philosophy of continuous training and development has resulted in a broad technical knowledge base and skill-set. Our methodologies are driven by our Quality Management System, under the guidance of ISO-9001:2008 and TickIT to which the company has been accredited since 1994 – being one of the first Software Houses in Europe to do so.
